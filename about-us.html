Our mission. 

HoW IT all started

We love to travel, and we equally love discovering local places, especially in the coffee and food business. So whenever we are traveling, we find ourselves researching online for the best small businesses representing the local culture and offering the superior quality that we are used to from our favorite shops in our hometown. Yet the current recommendation systems do not really facilitate this discovery, as the collection of places comes unfiltered, and from foreigners who may have a completely different taste. 
We thought there has to be a better way of recommendation systems providing actual value to consumers. 

We at Avantoria believe that if there is one group who knows great places offering a superior quality and splendid experience, then these are the business owners themselves. If we would receive a list of places our favorite bloggers and chefs love in the city that we are visiting, there is no doubt that we will be delighted at all times. In a world that becomes more and more digital, ultimately our mission is to use the digital environment to enhance the physical touchpoint: by creating more unique experiences and by shaping a new type of community among experts and followers.
